about us

our mission our vision

The Continuum Indepth Consultants (CIC) is one of the top regional group of consultants’ made up of Forensic Accountants, Professional accountants, Practicing Auditors who are Finance Experts and IT Security & Digital Forensic Experts, to provide unrivalled service to consumer public and organizations both public and private.

As CIC we know that technical excellence alone is not enough and that you want to work with advisors who share your values, fit seamlessly into your working culture, provide integrated and holistic business solutions, having an in-depth understanding of your business. We work exceptionally hard to appreciate and understand our clientele needs and requirements, and in adherence with the firm’s vision, provide near-seamless and practical solutions that will achieve our customers’ short objectives and long-term ambitions. Our approach is to find the answers to the questions behind the client’s main challenge(s) and explore the reasons that underline a specific inquiry. By working this way, we aim to develop a deep understanding of our clients’ businesses, and endeavor to provide useful and relevant advice on how to engineer business growth.

OUR TEAM EXPERIENCE

Continuum Indepth Consultants (CIC) conducts financial audit, technology audit and IT security assessments for a variety of industries. We work collaboratively to help our clients understand the risks at their organization and how to address them.

CIC’s management team maintains industry-relevant certifications such as the Certified Information System Auditor (CISA), Certified Information System Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), Certified Forensic Examiner (CFE) and Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and memberships to relevant professional groups such as International Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA), EC-Council and the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE). All are highly skilled in various aspects of risk management, financial audit, technology and areas surrounding regulatory compliance and information security.
